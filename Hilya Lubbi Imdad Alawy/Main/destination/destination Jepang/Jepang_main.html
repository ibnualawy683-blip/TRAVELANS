Tokyo(東京)

Description: A modern capital city featuring the Tokyo Skytree (東京スカイツリー) tower, Senso-ji Temple (浅草寺), and the bustling Shibuya (渋谷) district. Enjoy a blend of traditional culture and cutting-edge technology in the city that never sleeps.

Popular Activities: Visit Tokyo Disneyland (東京ディズニーランド), stroll around Akihabara (秋葉原), and eat sushi at Tsukiji (築地).

Kyoto(京都)

Description: A historic city featuring ancient temples like Fushimi Inari Shrine (伏見稲荷大社) and Kinkaku-ji (金閣寺). Experience peace amid the natural beauty and traditional Japanese architecture.

Popular Activities: Explore Arashiyama Bamboo Grove (嵐山竹林), visit Kiyomizu-dera Temple (清水寺), and ride the shinkansen (新幹線) train.

Osaka(大阪)

Description: A food city featuring Osaka Castle (大阪城), the Kaiyukan (海遊館) aquarium, and the famous Dotonbori (道頓堀) street. A paradise for food and entertainment lovers with an energetic atmosphere.

Popular Activities:Eat takoyaki (たこ焼き), visit Universal Studios Japan (ユニバーサル・スタジオ・ジャパン), and explore Osaka Castle (大阪城).

Hiroshima(広島)

Description: A city featuring the Peace Memorial Park (平和記念公園) and the atomic bomb memorial museum. A place that inspires reflection on peace, surrounded by natural beauty.

Popular Activities: Visit the Peace Memorial, take a ferry to Miyajima (宮島), and explore Hiroshima's parks.

Nara(奈良)

Description: A city featuring Todai-ji Temple (東大寺) and wild, feedable deer. A unique experience of interacting with wildlife amidst historic sites.

Popular Activities: Feed the deer at Nara Park (奈良公園), visit Todai-ji Temple (東大寺), and explore the Kasugayama Primeval Forest (春日山原始林)..
