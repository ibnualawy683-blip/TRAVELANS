Niagara Falls

Niagara Falls is one of Canada’s most iconic natural wonders, located on the border between Ontario and the United States. The powerful waterfalls attract millions of visitors each year, offering boat tours, observation decks, and a stunning night light show.

Banff National Park

Nestled in the heart of the Canadian Rockies, Banff National Park offers breathtaking mountain views, crystal-clear lakes, and diverse wildlife. It’s a paradise for hiking, skiing, and nature photography enthusiasts from around the world.

Old Quebec City

Old Quebec City is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its cobblestone streets, European-style architecture, and historical charm. The area offers cozy cafés, street performers, and the majestic Château Frontenac overlooking the St. Lawrence River.
