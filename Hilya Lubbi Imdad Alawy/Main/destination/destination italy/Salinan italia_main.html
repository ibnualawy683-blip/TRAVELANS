Tower of Pisa

The Tower of Pisa, also known as the Leaning Tower of Pisa, is one of the world's most famous landmarks, located in the city of Pisa, Italy. With its unique tilt, the tower has become a major attraction for tourists from all over the world.

Alpen Mountains

The Alpine region has a strong cultural identity. Traditional practices such as farming, cheesemaking, and woodworking still thrive in Alpine villages.

Amalfi Coast

The Amalfi Coast is famed for its gorgeous beaches, where clear turquoise waters lap limestone cliffs and sugary white sands. Centuries-old landmarks straddle the shore in the namesake town, with pastel-coloured paintwork evoking a vibrant coastal scene.
