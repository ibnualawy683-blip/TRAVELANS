Statue of Liberty

This majestic statue of a woman with a torch in her hand, reaching toward the sky, has become the personification of American freedom. The crown on her head has seven rays, signifying the seven continents and seven oceans (according to Western geographic tradition). In her other hand, she holds a plate with the date of the Declaration of Independence engraved on it.

Central Park

The green zone stretches 4 km long and 800 meters wide. The park opened in 1859. Tens of thousands of workers spent 20 years improving the area. Around 5 million trees were planted, and the soil was brought from ecologically clean areas.
