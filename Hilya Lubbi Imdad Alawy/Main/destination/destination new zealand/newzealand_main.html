Auckland

Auckland pairs city lights with harbor blues and ancient cones of green, all within minutes of each other.

Rotorua (North Island)

Steam rises from hot pools, turquoise lakes glow, and Maori stories echo through ancient forests.

Matamata (Waikato, North Island)

Emerald hills cradle tiny round doors, opening straight into the beloved fantasy world of film.

Queenstown (South Island)

Snowy peaks mirror a crystal lake, daring every visitor to jump, ride, or soar above the view.

Christchurch (South Island)

Stone towers, fragrant gardens, and slow river boats frame a city wrapped in quiet English charm.

Tekapo (Mackenzie Region, South Island)

Glacier fed water shines neon blue by day, then reflects endless stars once night blankets the sky.
